SHANNA CHAPUT
Certified Pilates Teacher - Holistic BodyWorx

Starting at the age of 3, Shanna's life of fitness & activity was born. Starting first in gymnastics then moving to dance, Shanna was a competitive dancer at the National Level for 13 years winning multiple awards & scholarships. Jazz, Tap, Ballet, Acro & Modern brought her all over North America through her childhood & teens.

At the age of 15, Shanna's family Doctor found that she had scoliosis & due to the severity, there was no other option but surgery. With a positive mind & drive to get back to fitness & dance, Shanna recovered & danced for another year. However, her flexibility was lost & stiffness in the legs were apparent. Shanna knew she had to let go of dance & move into a new passion.

After a couple years of gyms & spin classes, Shanna discovered Pilates and started working at Holistic Body Worx in early 2009. Not only with a 10 year life in Pilates, but also doing personal training to ensure that her Scoliosis would never give her an issue.

In 2018, Shanna took the Pilates Teachers Training course at Holistic Body Worx and she is now certified & currently teaches twice a week.  Shanna knows proper positioning & alignment due to her training as well as her own personal knowledge of the spine & core. She knows that to gain the greatest results, one must do each exercise properly and focus on both the mind & body.  Shanna lives by the knowledge that fitness is a lifestyle and not a trend or fad.
