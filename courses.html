 Yoga and Pilates Teacher Certification Programs

Holistic Bodyworx Courses

Yoga Teacher Training Certification Course 

Our award winning Yoga Teacher training course is a 230 hour Certification course taught in the Hatha Yoga tradition. Holistic Bodyworx is a registered school with the Yoga Alliance. The Yoga Alliance is the largest Yoga standardization registrar in North America.

Pilates Teacher Training Certification Course 

Our Pilates Teacher training course is a 105 hour Certification Course. The goal of our course is to teach you to become a dynamic teacher while at the same time providing the opportunity to explore and discover the power within yourself both as a teacher and as an individual.
